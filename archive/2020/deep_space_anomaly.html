Deepspaceanomaly, 2020

deepspaceanomaly was made in response to a collaborative open-call organised by Equisite Corpos. The open-call was to create a series of videos which stitched together each video responding to the last 3 seconds of the previos video, similar in nature to the kids drawing game of the same name Equisite Corpse.
